## **Petal-Paradise Website Project – Complete Overview**

### **Introduction**

**Petal Paradise** is a modern and responsive flower shop website developed using **HTML5 and CSS3**. The website is designed to provide customers with an attractive online platform where they can explore a variety of fresh flowers, bouquets, and decorative flower pots. The project focuses on creating a visually appealing, easy-to-use, and mobile-friendly interface that enhances the user experience.

---

### **Project Description**

The website serves as an online flower shop that showcases different flower products and their prices. It allows customers to browse flower collections, view flower pot designs, check pricing details, and contact the shop for inquiries or orders. The design uses a beautiful lavender color theme, smooth navigation, and attractive product cards to create a professional look.

The website consists of multiple sections that help users easily access information about the shop and its products.

---

### **Modules of the Project**

#### **1. Home Section**

The Home section acts as the landing page of the website. It welcomes visitors with an attractive heading, brief description, and a "Shop Now" button that directs users to the flower collection.

#### **2. About Us Section**

This section provides information about Safiya Flower Shop, its services, and the types of flower products available. It helps customers understand the purpose and offerings of the business.

#### **3. Flower Collection Section**

The Flower Collection section displays different types of flower bouquets such as:

* Rose Bouquet
* Lily Bouquet
* Sunflower Bouquet
* Tulip Bouquet
* Mixed Flower Bouquet
* Orchid Bouquet

Each product card contains an image, description, price, and an order button.

#### **4. Flower Pots Collection**

This section showcases decorative flower pots, including:

* Ceramic Pot
* Designer Pot
* Garden Pot

Customers can view the products and their prices through attractive product cards.

#### **5. Pricing Section**

The Pricing section contains a table displaying all available products and their respective prices, helping customers quickly compare costs.

#### **6. Contact Section**

The Contact section includes:

* Customer contact form
* Phone number
* Email address

This allows customers to communicate with the flower shop for orders, feedback, and inquiries.
